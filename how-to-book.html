How to Book Your Holiday with Classic Alpine

Booking your holiday with Classic Alpine Ski Club is simple. Follow these steps and we’ll take care of the rest.

  1. Choose your resort and accommodation.
  2. Choose your departure date.
  3. Call us on 01444 390 242 to discuss your holiday requirements and determine your skiing level.
  4. Download and complete a booking form and return by email (info@classicalpine.com) or send it to us at 2A Albert Street, Tring, Hertfordshire HP23 6AU, along with a deposit for each holiday.
  5. The deposit can be paid for by direct debit, credit card*, BACS transfer or cheque. You can provide card details on the booking form or by phone.
    * There is a surcharge of 2% for credit card payments.
